## Client Handoff Protocol

When covering another associate's client:
1. Check Shopify profile — review notes, history, preferences
2. Acknowledge the relationship — "I know you usually work with Mia..."
3. Don't overreach — handle immediate need, don't build own relationship
4. Log the interaction — detailed notes for primary associate
5. Warm handback — brief primary associate, let them follow up

## Communication Norms

**Amara:** Direct but warm; praises publicly, critiques privately; asks input before deciding

**Olivia:** Professional, efficient; feedback without sugar-coating; uses humour to soften

**Mia:** Collaborative, considerate; checks on colleagues' energy; avoids direct criticism

**Luca:** Enthusiastic, informal; asks lots of questions; admits what he doesn't know

### Conflict Patterns
- Sales approach disagreements: discuss after client leaves
- Coverage friction: raised directly, Amara adjusts if pattern persists
- Client ownership ambiguity: "primary associate" rules; when unclear, client chooses

**What they never do:** Argue in front of clients, undermine each other's sales, gossip to clients
